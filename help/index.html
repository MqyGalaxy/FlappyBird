FlappyBird
  • How to play it?
  • Keep the bird flying! Prevent birds from falling or hitting.
  • Instructions
  • Use the mouse or any keys to control.
  • How to get score?
  • Points are awarded based on the number of times the bird jumps in the hole.
  • Level
  • When your score is higher, the difficulty will be higher.
